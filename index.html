Selling Your Home: A Step-by-Step Guide

by Admin

23 June, 2023

Start by determining your budget and getting pre-approved for a mortgage. This helps you know exactly what you can afford before you start touring homes.

Focus on decluttering, deep cleaning, and making minor repairs. Neutral paint colors and good lighting can also significantly increase your home's appeal.

During closing, you'll sign legal documents, pay closing costs, and officially transfer the property title. Your agent will guide you through every signature.

On average, it takes 30 to 60 days from finding a home to closing, depending on financing and inspections.

You will typically need proof of income, bank statements, tax returns, and a valid ID to ensure a seamless verification process
